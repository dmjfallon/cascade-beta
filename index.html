🌊 About

Most mortgage calculators handle a single loan. This calculator models two mortgages (using monthly compounding calculations), applies overpayments to the highest rate first, and compares this to paying off the mortgages separately.

🚫 What’s Not Considered
  • Early repayment charges
  • Overpayment limits
  • Tax considerations
  • Rate changes or refinancing
  • Lender-specific rules
  • Comparison with investing extra money instead
  • And so on...
⚠️ Results are estimates
  • For illustrative purposes only, not guarantees. Always seek professional advice before making significant financial decisions.
  • Nothing is stored, calculations run in browser.
  • Anonymous usage analytics are used to improve the tool.
